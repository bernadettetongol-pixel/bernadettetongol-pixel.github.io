About

15 years learning
the UAE market
from the inside

I didn't learn the UAE retail market from a textbook. My expertise was built from the showroom floor, the lease negotiation table, and the boardroom. I worked my way from Sales Executive to managing brand partnerships and commercial leases for some of the UAE's most prominent retail groups.

I've negotiated directly with EMAAR, ALDAR, MERAAS, and MAF. I've managed relationships with some of the world's most demanding luxury brands. I've built BD pipelines, closed contracts, and led customer experience transformations across more than 15 years in this market.

Today I bring that experience to businesses that need senior-level guidance without the cost or commitment of a permanent hire. Based in Dubai, available across the UAE.
